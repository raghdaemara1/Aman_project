SECTION 1 — COVERED SERVICES
================================================================================

The following services are covered under this Comprehensive Health Insurance policy:

1. Hospitalization
   - Inpatient admission at any accredited network hospital
   - Up to 90 days per policy year
   - Includes room and board, nursing care, and standard medications

2. Emergency Surgery
   - All emergency surgical procedures performed at accredited facilities
   - Pre-authorization required for non-emergency surgeries within 48 hours

3. Outpatient Consultations
   - Up to 20 specialist consultations per policy year
   - General practitioner visits: unlimited
   - Referrals from network GPs required for specialist visits

4. Laboratory Tests and Diagnostics
   - Blood tests, urine analysis, and standard diagnostic panels
   - Radiology: X-rays, ultrasound, MRI (with prior authorization for MRI)
   - Covered at 100% when conducted at network laboratories

5. Prescription Medications
   - Covered up to EGP 2,000 per policy year
   - Only medications on the AMAN approved formulary list
   - Generic substitutions required where available

6. Maternity Coverage
   - Prenatal care, delivery, and postnatal care
   - Available after a 12-month waiting period from policy start date
   - Covers normal and caesarean delivery at network hospitals
   - Newborn care covered for the first 30 days after birth

SECTION 3 — EXCLUSIONS
================================================================================

The following conditions and services are NOT covered under this policy:

1. Pre-existing Conditions
   Any medical condition diagnosed, treated, or symptomatic before the policy
   start date of January 1, 2025, is excluded from coverage for the first
   24 months of the policy.

2. Cosmetic and Elective Procedures
   Any procedure performed for aesthetic, cosmetic, or elective reasons that
   is not medically necessary. This includes but is not limited to: rhinoplasty,
   liposuction, Botox, hair transplants, and dental veneers.

3. Dental and Optical Care
   Routine dental treatments, fillings, extractions, orthodontics, and all
   optical services including prescription glasses and contact lenses.
   Emergency dental treatment due to accident is covered.

4. Mental Health Treatment
   Psychiatric consultations, psychological therapy, inpatient mental health
   facilities, and prescription medications for mental health conditions.

5. Injuries Resulting from Extreme Sports
   Any injury sustained while participating in extreme or hazardous sports
   including: skydiving, bungee jumping, rock climbing, motor racing,
   paragliding, and contact martial arts competitions.

6. Treatment Outside Egypt
   Medical treatment received outside the Arab Republic of Egypt is not covered
   except in genuine life-threatening emergencies. Reimbursement for emergency
   overseas treatment requires submission of all original receipts within 30 days
   of return to Egypt.

SECTION 4 — CLAIMS PROCEDURE
================================================================================

For cashless treatment at network hospitals:
1. Present your AMAN insurance card and national ID
2. Hospital contacts AMAN for pre-authorization (within 2 hours)
3. Treatment proceeds upon authorization

For reimbursement claims:
1. Submit claim form within 45 days of treatment
2. Include original invoices, prescriptions, and medical reports
3. Payment processed within 15 business days of complete documentation
