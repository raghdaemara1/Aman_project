SECTION 1 — AMAN FINANCE TERMS AND CONDITIONS
================================================================================

1.1 Nature of Agreement
This is a Sharia-compliant Murabaha installment finance agreement regulated by
the Egyptian Financial Regulatory Authority (FRA) under Law No. 176 of 2018.
Aman Consumer Finance purchases the product and resells it to the customer at
the agreed total price payable in monthly installments.

1.2 Ownership Transfer
Legal ownership of the financed product transfers to the customer upon full
repayment of all installments. Until final payment is made, Aman Consumer
Finance retains a security interest in the product.

1.3 Customer Obligations
The customer is obligated to:
- Make monthly payments on or before the due date each month
- Maintain the financed product in good condition
- Not sell or transfer the product before full repayment without written consent
- Notify Aman immediately of any change in address or contact details

================================================================================
SECTION 2 — LATE PAYMENT AND PENALTIES
================================================================================

2.1 Late Payment Fee
A late payment administrative fee of EGP 75 per month will be charged for
any installment not paid within 7 calendar days of the due date.

2.2 Consecutive Missed Payments
If the customer misses 3 or more consecutive installments, Aman Consumer
Finance reserves the right to:
- Declare the entire outstanding balance immediately due
- Initiate legal proceedings for debt recovery
- Report the default to the I-Score credit bureau

2.3 Bounced Direct Debit
A fee of EGP 150 will be charged for each failed direct debit attempt
due to insufficient funds.

================================================================================
SECTION 3 — EARLY SETTLEMENT
================================================================================

3.1 Full Early Settlement
The customer may settle the remaining balance in full at any time.
An early settlement fee of 1% of the outstanding principal applies
if settlement occurs within the first 12 months of the contract.
No early settlement fee applies after 12 months.

3.2 Partial Prepayment
Partial prepayments are accepted in multiples of EGP 1,000.
Partial prepayments reduce the remaining number of installments;
the monthly installment amount remains unchanged.
